The digital age has forged two vital weapons for navigating its vast information seas: data analytics and cyber security. Data analytics empowers organizations to dive deep into vast datasets, extracting valuable insights that drive informed decisions, optimize operations, and unlock competitive advantages. From healthcare to finance, data analysis fuels innovation, boosting efficiency, customer satisfaction, and the overall digital landscape.


The 9th edition of the International Conference on Data Analytics and Cyber Security (DACS) aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, and industry experts from around the globe to explore the latest advancements and emerging trends in these two interconnected fields.
