International Conference on Data Analytics and Cyber Security (DACS)

DACS Introduction

In today's data-driven world, data analytics and cyber security are two of the most critical fields of study. Data analytics plays a vital role in extracting valuable insights from large and complex datasets, enabling organizations to make informed decisions, optimize operations, and gain a competitive edge. On the other hand, cyber security is paramount in protecting sensitive data and critical infrastructure from cyberattacks, ensuring the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of information systems.

The DACS 2026 conference is the 9th edition and the 2nd as an independent conference. The conference in the previous year (7th DACS-2024) was held at IIM Bodh Gaya, India. Prior to becoming an independent event, this conference was conducted as an International Symposium in conjunction with top-tier conferences. The specific details of these earlier versions are as follows:

  • The 9th DACS 2026 will be at IIIT Guwahati, India.
  • The 8th DACS 2025 was held in SRM University-AP, India.
  • The 7th DACS 2024 was held in IIM Bodh Gaya, India.
  • International Workshop on Artificial Intelligence for Intelligent Network Management (the 5th AINet 2022 and the 6th AINet 2023), both held in Torino, Italy, in conjunction with IEEE COMPSAC.
  • The 4th AINet 2021, held in Torino, Italy, in conjunction with IEEE ISPA.
  • International Symposium on Security, Privacy, and Trust in the Internet of Things (1st SPTIoT 2017 in Sydney, Australia; 2nd SPTIoT 2018 in New York, USA; and 3rd SPTIoT 2019 in Rotorua, New Zealand) in conjunction with IEEE TrustCom.
